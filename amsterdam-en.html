Amsterdam

The city of canals, creativity, and open-minded spirit.

About Amsterdam

Amsterdam, the capital of the Netherlands, is a vibrant city known for its artistic heritage, elaborate canal system, and narrow houses with gabled facades — a legacy of the 17th-century Golden Age. The city combines history, innovation, and freedom in a truly unique way.

Main Attractions

  • Rijksmuseum – showcasing masterpieces from Dutch Golden Age artists like Rembrandt.
  • Anne Frank House – a moving historical site telling the story of Anne Frank during WWII.
  • Van Gogh Museum – home to the world’s largest collection of Van Gogh’s works.
  • Canal Cruise – the best way to explore Amsterdam’s beauty is from the water.
  • Vondelpark – a relaxing green space in the heart of the city.

Culture & Lifestyle

Amsterdam celebrates creativity and freedom. Its people are friendly, open, and value sustainability. Bicycles are everywhere — the city is built for riders, not cars.

Local Cuisine

  • Stroopwafel – caramel syrup waffle cookies.
  • Bitterballen – deep-fried meat snacks perfect with mustard.
  • Haring – raw herring fish, a traditional Dutch delicacy.
  • Poffertjes – fluffy mini pancakes served with butter and sugar.
